Entry-Level Scanner

The SmartScan P1 is a cutting-edge OBD2 scanner designed for personal use, integrating advanced AI technology to provide accurate and efficient vehicle diagnostics. With its user-friendly interface, the SmartScan P1 connects seamlessly to your smartphone via Bluetooth, offering real-time data and insights. The AI engine analyzes the vehicle’s data, identifying potential issues and suggesting solutions, making it easy for car owners to understand and address problems without needing extensive mechanical knowledge. Whether it’s a simple check engine light or a more complex issue, the SmartScan P1 empowers users to maintain their vehicles with confidence.
